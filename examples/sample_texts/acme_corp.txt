Acme Corporation — Company Overview

Acme Corp is a rapidly growing cloud infrastructure company headquartered in San Francisco, California. Founded in 2014 by a team of ex-Google and ex-AWS engineers, the company has carved out a strong niche in the DevOps automation space, helping mid-market enterprises manage their cloud workloads at scale.

The company currently employs approximately 1,200 people across offices in San Francisco, New York, Austin, and London. Over the past fiscal year, Acme reported revenues in the range of $80 million to $100 million, representing 34% year-over-year growth — one of the highest growth rates in its peer group.

Acme's flagship product suite includes three main offerings: CloudSync, a real-time infrastructure synchronization platform; DataBridge, a zero-downtime data migration tool; and AutoScale, an AI-powered autoscaling engine that dynamically adjusts compute resources based on predictive traffic modeling.

The company recently closed a $45 million Series C round led by Sequoia Capital, with participation from a16z and existing investors. The funds are earmarked for international expansion into the APAC region and deeper investment in their AI/ML pipeline capabilities.

Acme Corp operates on a SaaS subscription model and boasts a Net Revenue Retention (NRR) of 127%, signaling strong expansion revenue within its existing customer base. Enterprise customers include Fortune 500 companies in financial services, healthcare, and e-commerce. The company is widely considered a top IPO candidate for 2026.
