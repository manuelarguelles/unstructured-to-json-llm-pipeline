Initech Capital Partners is a mid-market focused private equity firm headquartered in New York, New York. Founded in 2007 by seasoned operators and former investment bankers, the firm manages approximately $2.8 billion in assets under management (AUM) across three active funds.

The firm specializes in control buyouts of B2B software, fintech infrastructure, and AI-enabled services companies. Initech's investment thesis centers on partnering with founder-led businesses generating $5M to $30M in EBITDA that are ready to scale with operational support and strategic capital. Target deal sizes typically range from $25 million to $150 million in enterprise value.

Initech's recent deal history includes the acquisition of DataCo (a data pipeline SaaS company) in 2022 for $62 million, the merger of SyncSoft and CloudLayer to create a unified cloud management platform in 2023, and a minority growth investment in AI analytics firm VectorSight in late 2024.

Current portfolio priorities for 2025 and 2026 include AI infrastructure tooling, revenue operations software, healthcare data management, and compliance automation. The firm is actively sourcing platforms and add-on acquisitions in these areas and prefers businesses with strong recurring revenue, net retention above 110%, and a clear path to $50M ARR.

Initech operates with a lean team of 22 investment professionals and has offices in New York and San Francisco.
